## Seed Targets To Research

These are categories, not verified leads. Use them to build the first list:

- Dental clinics advertising first consultation or implant valuation.
- Aesthetic clinics advertising facial/body treatments.
- Physiotherapy clinics with WhatsApp booking.
- Real estate agencies with property visit requests.
- Legal or tax advisory firms offering first consultation.
