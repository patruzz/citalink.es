### Product Agent

First pilot workflow should be one channel only.

Recommended first workflow:

- Web form or WhatsApp request arrives.
- CitaLink responds quickly.
- CitaLink asks 2-4 qualification questions.
- CitaLink proposes appointment slots.
- Human review approves first messages.
- Dashboard tracks response time and appointments recovered.

### Compliance Agent

Growth must stay low-volume and specific until legal basis is clear.

Rules:

- Public business channels only.
- Manual review before first outreach.
- Opt-out sentence in follow-up if needed.
- No sensitive health data in outbound messages.
