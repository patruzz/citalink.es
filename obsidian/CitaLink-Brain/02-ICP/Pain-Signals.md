# Pain Signals

Use these signals while researching accounts.

## Strong Signals

- "Pedir cita" CTA goes to a form instead of an instant booking flow.
- WhatsApp is prominent but unmanaged-looking.
- Phone is the main CTA and the business has long opening hours.
- Instagram is active and comments ask for price or appointments.
- Reviews mention waiting, communication, or difficulty booking.
- Ads, promotions, or landing pages suggest paid acquisition spend.
- Multiple specialists or locations create coordination complexity.

## Questions To Validate In Audit

- How many appointment requests arrive each month?
- What channels create the most noise?
- Who responds first and how fast?
- How many requests never get a second touch?
- How many appointments are confirmed but not attended?
- What is the average value of an attended appointment?

## Bad Fit Signals

- No visible digital channel.
- Very low appointment value.
- Franchise location with no local decision power.
- No recurring appointment workflow.
- No willingness to measure current leakage.
