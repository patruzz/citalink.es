# ICP - Clinicas Esteticas

## Why This Segment First

Aesthetic clinics often receive high-intent but fragile demand through Instagram, WhatsApp, calls, forms, and ads. Response speed and follow-up quality can decide whether a consultation turns into revenue.

## Best Fit Signals

- First consultation or valuation CTA.
- High-ticket services such as facial aesthetics, laser, body treatments, or medical aesthetics.
- Instagram and WhatsApp are prominent.
- Promotions, campaigns, or seasonal offers.
- Reviews and before/after content indicate active demand.

## Pain Signals

- Messages likely arrive outside business hours.
- Multiple channels with no single owner.
- Lead quality varies and needs qualification.
- No-shows or reprogramming likely consume staff time.

## First Workflow

- Instagram/WhatsApp request to valuation.
- Web form to treatment-interest qualification.
- Reminder sequence before valuation.

## Decision Maker

- Clinic owner.
- Aesthetic doctor or director.
- Clinic manager.
- Marketing lead.

## Message Angle

> Si recuperais 3-5 valoraciones al mes que hoy se quedan frias en WhatsApp o Instagram, el piloto se paga solo muy rapido.
