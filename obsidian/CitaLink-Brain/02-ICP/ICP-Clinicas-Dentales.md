# ICP - Clinicas Dentales

## Why This Segment First

Dental clinics are a strong first-customer wedge because they combine high appointment value, visible demand, repeatable workflows, and fast decision cycles.

## Best Fit Signals

- Implant, orthodontics, aesthetics, or first-visit valuation offers.
- WhatsApp, phone, web form, Instagram, or Google Business as appointment channels.
- Multiple doctors, specialties, or locations.
- Active reviews and active acquisition.
- Clear "pedir cita" or "primera consulta" CTA.
- Owner, clinic director, or manager visible.

## Pain Signals

- WhatsApp shown as the main CTA but no promise of response time.
- Web form without immediate booking.
- Long opening hours or emergency language.
- Reception team likely handling phone, WhatsApp, and walk-ins together.
- Reviews mention waiting, communication, or scheduling friction.

## First Workflow

Start with one of these:

- WhatsApp request to valuation booked.
- Web form to first appointment confirmed.
- Missed call to callback and slot proposal.

## Decision Maker

- Clinic owner.
- Clinic director.
- Operations manager.
- Marketing manager for clinics with active acquisition.
