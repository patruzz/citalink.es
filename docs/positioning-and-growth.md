# CitaLink positioning and growth plan

## Decision

CitaLink should not position itself as a generic CRM, calendar tool, chatbot, or AI SDR platform.

The sharpest category is:

> CitaLink recovers lost appointments for businesses that sell through booked meetings.

This keeps the promise close to money, easy to understand, and directly connected to the current product: lead capture, fast response, qualification, scheduling, reminders, human review, and traceability.

## Ideal first customer

Start with appointment-heavy businesses where a missed response has visible revenue impact:

- Private clinics, dental, aesthetics, wellness, and elective healthcare.
- Real estate agencies with inbound visit requests.
- Professional services that sell first consultations.
- Technical services with urgent requests and route planning.

The recommended wedge is clinics, dental, and aesthetics because the language of "consulta", "valoración", "no-show", "agenda", and "recordatorio" already matches the product. After proof, expand to other sectors with the same operating model.

## Differentiation

### Against calendars

Calendars help people pick a slot. CitaLink manages the messy work before and after the slot: respond, qualify, propose, confirm, remind, reschedule, and log the conversation.

### Against CRMs

CRMs store pipeline state. CitaLink creates clean pipeline state from real conversations and pushes the next action into the team workflow.

### Against chatbots

Bots answer questions. CitaLink owns a revenue workflow with business rules, human review, consent, and auditability.

### Against AI SDR tools

Most AI SDR tools are framed around outbound prospecting. CitaLink should lead with inbound intent recovery: people already asked for help, so the fastest path to value is rescuing those opportunities.

## Product narrative

The website and product should tell one story:

1. A lead arrives from phone, form, email, or WhatsApp.
2. CitaLink responds before the lead goes cold.
3. The agent qualifies intent, urgency, service, location, and availability.
4. It proposes real calendar slots and confirms the appointment.
5. It sends reminders and manages reprogramming.
6. It leaves a full record for the team: state, next action, consent, and channel.

## Website funnel

The primary CTA should be "Pedir auditoría de citas", not only "Pedir demo".

The audit should ask:

- How many monthly leads or consultation requests arrive?
- Which channels generate the most friction?
- What percentage is not answered quickly?
- What is the average value of an attended appointment?
- Where do no-shows and reprogramming happen?

The output is a short opportunity estimate and a recommended first automation.

## Product-led sales loop

To make the product sell itself, the first experience should feel useful before a sales call:

1. Visitor uses the appointment leakage calculator.
2. Visitor requests an audit with prefilled context.
3. CitaLink creates a demo request and an internal AI action.
4. Dashboard shows estimated leak, recommended workflow, and next step.
5. Demo call starts from the prospect's numbers instead of a generic tour.
6. The pilot is sold as "recover appointments in this workflow first".

## Packaging

Keep pricing simple, but sell the implementation path:

- Starter: one channel and one calendar for a focused workflow.
- Growth: multi-channel intake plus reminders and CRM handoff.
- Pro: custom agents, multiple locations, advanced compliance, and integrations.

For early customers, lead with a paid pilot:

> 30-day appointment recovery pilot: connect one channel, one agenda, one sector playbook, and measure citas recuperadas.

## Core KPIs

The dashboard should prioritize:

- First response time.
- Leads without response.
- Leads converted to booked appointment.
- Booked appointments attended.
- No-shows prevented or reprogrammed.
- Value of recovered appointments.
- Actions awaiting human approval.
- Opt-outs and do-not-contact events.

## Messaging rules

- Say "recuperar citas perdidas" before saying "IA".
- Use "consulta", "valoración", "visita", and "cita" by sector.
- Avoid claiming guaranteed revenue results.
- Show the workflow before showing the technology.
- Make compliance and human review a trust signal, not a footnote.
